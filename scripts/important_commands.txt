Important commands

** To use models/research **
go to models/research
$ export PYTHONPATH=$PYTHONPATH:`pwd`:`pwd`/slim

** Train a model **
$ python3 object_detection/model_main.py \
--model_dir=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/trainings/ssd_mobilenet_train_blurry \
--pipeline_config_path==/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/trainings/ssd_mobilenet_train_blurry/ssd_mobilenet_v1_fpn_shared_box_predictor_640x640_coco14_sync.config

** Export inference graph from trained model **
$ python3 object_detection/export_inference_graph.py \
--input_type=image_tensor \
--pipeline_config_path=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/trainings/ssd_mobilenet_train_blurry/ssd_mobilenet_v1_fpn_shared_box_predictor_640x640_coco14_sync.config \
--trained_checkpoint_prefix=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/trainings/ssd_mobilenet_train_blurry/model.ckpt-25000 \
--output_directory=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/models_trained/ssd_mobilenet_trained_blurry_model/

** Generate inference tfrecord **
$ python3 object_detection/inference/infer_detections.py \
--input_tfrecord_paths=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/blurry_dataset/eval_data/eval.record \
--output_tfrecord_path=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/inferences/ssd_mobilenet_v1_blurry_eval \
--inference_graph=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/models_trained/ssd_mobilenet_trained_blurry_model/frozen_inference_graph.pb 

** Generate metrics.csv **
$ python3 object_detection/metrics/offline_eval_map_corloc.py \
--eval_dir=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/metrics/ssd_mobilenet_v1_trained_blurry_eval_metrics/ \
--eval_config_path=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/metrics/ssd_mobilenet_v1_trained_blurry_eval_metrics/eval_config.pbtxt \
--input_config_path=/media/nas/PeopleDetection/tensorflow/metrics/ssd_mobilenet_v1_trained_blurry_eval_metrics/input_config.pbtxt
